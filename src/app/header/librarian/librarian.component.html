WELCOME TO BIBLIO TECHIE

My name is Cori. I created Biblio Techie as a way to help organize reading lists and help boost motivation to bust those lists out. I was an avid reader when I was younger, but that slowly changed as I got older. A goal of mine is to start making a dent in my large physical and digital library like I use to do.
Biblio Techie helps keep track of not only your current library and what you have already read, but what books you are itching to add it it.
